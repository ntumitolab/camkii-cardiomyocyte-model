# Beta-adrenergic system

Activities are fitted to the steady-state activities in the Morroti model.

$$
\begin{align}
f_{PKACI} &= PKACI_0 + \frac{PKACI_{act}}{1 + \frac{PKACI_{KM}}{ISO}} \\
f_{PKACII} &= PKACII_0 + \frac{PKACII_{act}}{1 + \frac{PKACII_{KM}}{ISO}} \\
f_{PP1} &= PP1_0 + \frac{PP1_{act}}{ 1 + \frac{ISO}{PP1_{KI}}}  \\
f_{PLBp} &= PLBp_0 + \frac{PLBp_{act}}{1 + (\frac{PLBp_{KM}}{ISO})^{PLBp_{n}}} \\
f_{PLMp} &= PLMp_0 + \frac{PLMp_{act}}{1 + (\frac{PLMp_{KM}}{ISO})^{PLMp_{n}}} \\
TnI_{PKAp} &= TnIp_0 + \frac{TnIp_{act}}{1 + (\frac{TnIp_{KM}}{ISO})^{TnIp_{n}}} \\
LCCa_{PKAp} &= LCCap_0 + \frac{LCCap_{act}}{1 + \frac{LCCap_{KM}}{ISO}} \\
LCCb_{PKAp} &= LCCbp_0 + \frac{LCCbp_{act}}{1 + \frac{LCCbp_{KM}}{ISO}} \\
KUR_{PKAp} &= KURp_0 + \frac{KURp_{act}}{1 + \frac{KURp_{KM}}{ISO}} \\
RyR_{PKAp} &= RyRp_0 + \frac{RyRp_{act}}{1 + \frac{RyRp_{KM}}{ISO}} \\
\end{align}
$$

## Full model

- v1: b1AR <--> LR
- v2: LR + Gs <--> LRG
- v3: b1AR + Gs <--> RG
- v4: RG --> b1AR + GsaGTP + Gsby
- v5: LRG --> b1AR + GsaGTP + Gsby
- v6: GsaGTP --> GsaGDP
- v7: GsaGDP + Gsby --> Gs
- v8: LR <--> b1AR_S464
- v9: LRG --> b1AR_S464 + Gs
- v10: b1AR <--> b1AR_S301
- v11: LR --> b1AR_S301
- v12: LRG --> b1AR_S301 + Gs
- v13: AC + GsaGTP <--> AC_GsaGTP
- v14: AC_GsaGTP --> AC + GsaGDP
- v15: PDE <--> PDEp
- v16f: 0 --> cAMP
- v16r: cAMP --> 0
- v17: RC_I + cAMP <--> RCcAMP_I
- v18: RC_II + cAMP <--> RCcAMP_II
- v19: RCcAMP_I + cAMP <--> RCcAMPcAMP_I
- v20: RCcAMP_II + cAMP <--> RCcAMPcAMP_II
- v21: RCcAMPcAMP_I <--> RcAMPcAMP_I + PKACI
- v22: RCcAMPcAMP_II <--> RcAMPcAMP_II + PKACII
- v23: PKACI + PKI <--> PKACI_PKI
- v24: PKACII + PKI <--> PKACII_PKI
- v25: I1 <--> I1p
- v26: I1p + PP1 <--> I1p_PP1
- v27: PLB <=> PLBp
- v28: PLM <=> PLMp
- v29: TnI <=> TnIp
- v30: LCCa <=> LCCap
- v31: LCCb <=> LCCbp
- v32: KURn <=> KURp
- v33: RyRn <=> RyRp

$$
\begin{align}

v_2 &= kf_{LRG} \cdot \mathtt{LR} \cdot \mathtt{Gs} - kr_{LRG} \mathtt{LRG} \\

v_{27} &=  \frac{k_{PKA,PLB} \cdot \mathtt{PKACI} \cdot \mathtt{PLB}}{\mathtt{PLB} + Km_{PKA,PLB}} - \frac{k_{PP1,PLB} \cdot \mathtt{PP1} \cdot \mathtt{PLBp}}{\mathtt{PLBp} + Km_{PP1,PLB}} \\

{LCCbp}^{\prime} &= \epsilon \cdot \mathtt{LCCbp} \\
